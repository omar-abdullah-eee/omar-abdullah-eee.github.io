Forecast-Integrated MPC for Hydropower Generation & Reservoir Management at Karnaphuli Hydropower Station

Developed a 4-module pipeline for the Karnaphuli Hydropower Station (240 MW): rainfall prediction (LightGBM Classifier + CatBoost Regressor), 7-day inflow forecasting (HistGradientBoosting + ExtraTrees Regressor + Ridge Meta-Learner + Residual BiLSTM), water head forecasting (Stacked GRU), and a rolling MPC optimizer (CVXPY, 5-unit commitment) to maximize generation and minimize spillway discharge.

Supervised by Dr. Nipu Kumar Das — Professor & Head, Dept. of BME, CUET
